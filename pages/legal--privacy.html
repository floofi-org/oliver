1. Introduction

Welcome to Floofi Systems, where we provide online services and open-source software for public use. Since our founding, we have prioritized user privacy and legal compliance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information Collection

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email address (provided during registration), and IP address for legal compliance and to provide you with the service you have registered for.
  • Non-Personal Information: We gather data such as browser type, version, location, time and date, and pages visited for anonymous analytics purposes.

3. Use of Information

At Floofi Systems, we use the information we collect for the following purposes:
  • Personalized Experience: The personal information provided during registration, such as your name and email address, is used to customize your experience on our platform. This includes remembering your preferences and settings to improve the usability of our services.
  • Account Management and Recovery: We use your email address to communicate important information about your account, such as password recovery, updates, and notifications regarding your use of the services.
  • Legal Compliance: Your IP address is recorded as part of our compliance with applicable laws and regulations, which may require us to track and report certain activities. Please note that data collected for legal compliance purposes is retained for a period of three months, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.
  • Analytics and Improvements: Non-personal information, including browser type, version, location, and usage patterns, is analyzed to understand how users interact with our services. This data helps us identify areas for improvement, optimize our website's functionality, and enhance the overall user experience.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

At Floofi Systems, we prioritize your privacy and do not share your personal information with third parties, except under specific circumstances:
  • Explicit Consent: We will only share your personal information with third parties if you have given us explicit consent to do so.
  • Service Providers: In rare cases where third-party service providers are involved in the delivery of our services (such as hosting providers), they may have access to information solely to perform their tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal processes, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

To enhance transparency, Floofi Systems maintains a Warrant Canary on a separate page, which states whether we have received any secret government requests for user data, such as national security letters or FISA orders. Please refer to the Disclaimers page for the most up-to-date information.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously and implement a variety of measures to protect your information:
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard), ensuring that your information remains secure during transit.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are housed in secure facilities with strict physical security measures, including access controls and surveillance systems, to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits and monitor our systems in real-time to identify and address potential vulnerabilities. Our team is dedicated to maintaining up-to-date security practices and complying with industry standards.

6. User Rights

Floofi Systems respects your rights regarding your personal information and provides the following options:
  • Access: You can access your personal information at any time by logging into your account. This allows you to review the data we have on file.
  • Correction: If your personal information changes or you find any inaccuracies, you can update it through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information by contacting us through the contact form on our website or by using the option in your account settings. We will process your request promptly, subject to any legal obligations that may require us to retain certain information.
  • No Marketing Communications: We do not use your personal information for marketing purposes related to our public services.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our use of cookies and similar technologies is limited to essential functionalities:
  • Session Management: We use cookies primarily to manage user sessions, ensuring that you stay logged in as you navigate our site. This enhances your experience by providing a seamless and secure interaction with our services.
  • No Cross-Site Tracking: We do not engage in tracking users across different websites or use cookies for advertising purposes. Our focus is on maintaining a secure and private environment for our users.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Floofi Systems reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. While we aim to notify our users of significant changes, we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Continued use of our services after any modifications to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

Appendix: Use of user data for machine learning applications

Floofi Systems certifies that it has not, is current not and will not engage in data collection for the objective of training a machine learning model. Such operations will be done only after obtaining explicit consent from the affected users and allowing them to withdraw their consent at any time.
