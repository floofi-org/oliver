Floofi System's platforms and websites are managed by different services providers in different locations. The status page contains information about a service's provider.

Services hosted in the Ireland are provided by Microsoft Azure, a US-based hosting provider operating in the US and other countries, near or in Ireland. Please contact Microsoft for any issue related the use of these services.

Services hosted in the Netherlands are provided by Scaleway SAS, a French hosting provider operating in France and other countries, near or in Amsterdam. Please contact Scaleway for any issue related the use of these services.
